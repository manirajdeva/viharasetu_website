Spiti Valley

Spiti Valley — a high-altitude cold desert of ochre mountains, ancient monasteries, and villages among the highest inhabited places on Earth. We arrange acclimatised itineraries, monastery visits, and drives across Kunzum Pass to Chandratal.

Signature Experiences

  • Monastery circuit — Key, Dhankar, and Tabo
  • Overnight camping on the shores of Chandratal Lake
  • Crossing Kunzum Pass into the high villages of Langza, Komic, and Hikkim
