Himachal Hill Station Trail

One circuit through Himachal Pradesh's three classic escapes — colonial Shimla, the adventure valleys of Manali, and the riverside cafe culture of Kasol in the Parvati Valley. Pine forests, snow passes, and slow mountain mornings, all in a single package.

Signature Experiences

  • Colonial heritage walk in Shimla and a ride on the UNESCO-listed Kalka–Shimla toy train
  • Paragliding at Solang Valley and a snow-point excursion to Rohtang Pass
  • Riverside cafe evenings in Kasol and the trek to Kheerganga's hot springs
