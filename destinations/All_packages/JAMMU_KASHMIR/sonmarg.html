Sonmarg

Sonmarg — the 'Meadow of Gold' on the ancient Silk Road, where the Sindh River meanders past the Thajiwas Glacier and high Himalayan passes. We arrange glacier treks, pony rides, and scenic mountain drives.

Signature Experiences

  • Pony ride or trek to the Thajiwas Glacier
  • Scenic drive along the Sindh River towards Zoji La
  • Riverside camping and trout fishing
