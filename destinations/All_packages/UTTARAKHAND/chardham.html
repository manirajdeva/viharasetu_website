Chardham

Chardham — the sacred Himalayan circuit of Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath, and Badrinath. We arrange comfortable pilgrimage stays, local guides, and a well-paced itinerary through Uttarakhand's holiest shrines.

Signature Experiences

  • Guided darshan at all four Himalayan shrines — Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath & Badrinath
  • Trek and pony/palanquin support on the Kedarnath and Yamunotri routes
  • Riverside stays along the Alaknanda and Bhagirathi
