Valley of Flowers

Valley of Flowers — a UNESCO World Heritage alpine meadow in the Nanda Devi Biosphere Reserve, ablaze with hundreds of Himalayan flower species each monsoon. We arrange guided treks from Govindghat, stays at Ghangaria, and a combined visit to Hemkund Sahib.

Signature Experiences

  • Guided trek through the flower-carpeted meadows of the valley
  • Visit to the glacial lake and gurdwara at Hemkund Sahib
  • Overnight stay at the trailhead village of Ghangaria
