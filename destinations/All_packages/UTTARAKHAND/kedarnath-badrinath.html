Kedarnath & Badrinath

Kedarnath & Badrinath — the two most revered shrines of the Chardham, dedicated to Shiva and Vishnu deep in the Garhwal Himalayas. We arrange trek support, pony and palanquin arrangements, and comfortable stays along the route.

Signature Experiences

  • Trek or pony/palanquin ride to the Kedarnath Jyotirlinga temple
  • Darshan at Badrinath Temple on the banks of the Alaknanda
  • Overnight stays at Guptkashi and Joshimath along the route
