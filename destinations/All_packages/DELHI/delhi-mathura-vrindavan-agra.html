Delhi, Mathura, Vrindavan & Agra

A single trail through India's capital heritage and the heartland of Krishna devotion — imperial Delhi's forts and tombs, the temple lanes of Mathura and Vrindavan where the Bhakti movement still feels alive, and the marble grandeur of the Taj Mahal in Agra. Best enjoyed October through March, when cool, clear days make sightseeing and temple-hopping comfortable across all four stops.

Signature Experiences

  • Old & New Delhi heritage trail — Red Fort, Qutub Minar, Humayun's Tomb and the Lotus Temple
  • Krishna devotion circuit through Mathura's Janmabhoomi and Vrindavan's Banke Bihari & ISKCON temples
  • Sunrise viewing of the Taj Mahal with a certified heritage guide
