Vaishno Devi

Vaishno Devi — one of India's most visited pilgrimage sites, where devotees trek through the Trikuta Hills to the cave shrine of the Mother Goddess. We arrange trek support, pony and palanquin arrangements, and comfortable stays at Katra.

Signature Experiences

  • Guided trek or pony/palanquin ride from Katra to the Bhawan
  • Darshan at the sacred cave shrine of the Mother Goddess
  • Visit to the Bhairon Nath temple above the Bhawan
