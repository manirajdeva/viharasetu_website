Hampi

Hampi — the ruined splendour of the Vijayanagara Empire scattered across a boulder-strewn landscape. We arrange guided ruins tours, sunset viewpoints, and cycling trails through the ancient city.

Signature Experiences

  • Guided walk through Virupaksha Temple and Hampi Bazaar
  • Sunset at Hemakuta Hill and Matanga Hill
  • Coracle ride on the Tungabhadra River
