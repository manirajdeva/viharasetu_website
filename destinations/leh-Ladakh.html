Leh Ladakh

Leh Ladakh — high-altitude deserts, turquoise lakes, and centuries-old monasteries cradled in the Himalayas. We arrange acclimatised itineraries, monastery visits, and drives to Pangong and Nubra.

Signature Experiences

  • Monastery circuit — Thiksey, Hemis, and Shey Palace
  • Overnight stay on the shores of Pangong Tso
  • Nubra Valley crossing via Khardung La
